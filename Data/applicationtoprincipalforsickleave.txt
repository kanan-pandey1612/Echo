Here is a sample application to the principal for a sick leave:

[Student's Name]
[Class/Section]
[Date]

The Principal,
[School Name],
[School Address],

Subject: Application for Sick Leave

Respected Sir/Madam,

I am writing to request a sick leave for [date of leave] as I am not feeling well and am unable to attend school. I have been experiencing [briefly mention the reason for your absence, e.g. fever, stomach pain, etc.] and am under the advice of my parents to take a day's rest.

I apologize for any inconvenience this may cause and will make sure to catch up on my schoolwork as soon as I am feeling better. I will also ensure that all my assignments and projects are completed on time.

Please let me know if there are any issues or if there is any additional documentation required. I appreciate your understanding and support in this matter.

Thank you for your time and consideration.

Sincerely,

[Your Name]

[Your Signature]
